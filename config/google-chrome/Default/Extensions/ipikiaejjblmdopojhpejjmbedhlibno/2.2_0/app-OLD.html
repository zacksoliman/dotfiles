Hotkeys

  • k: rewind
  • h: hide controls
  • Space: play/pause
  • 1,2,3..9,0: seek to position in text
  • q: close Spreed window
  • l: reset
  • f: change font
  • "[" or "]": decrease/increase WPM

Buttons

  • Invert: invert Spreed colors
  • Highlight: highlight/unhighlight center letter
